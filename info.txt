<h3>What is Accordy Slider</h3>

Accordy Slider is a jQuery slider that comes in 4 different themes ready for your site. 

Accordy Slider comes with a number of options including custom links, custom text fields, custom content etc.

Accordy Slider is beautiful responsive as well and supports up to 10 slides of your choice.
